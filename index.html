I am a PhD student in Geography and Environmental Sustainability at the University of Oklahoma, working at the intersection of Earth observation, GeoAI, and climate–environment applications. My work focuses on monitoring environmental change and quantifying spatio-temporal drivers of urban and climate-related processes using satellite data and reproducible geospatial workflows.
